Oldschool Runescape

The (older, newer, better) massively multiplayer online grindy af medieval point and click childrens adventure game.

Adventure!

Giant dragons, angry dwarves, needy jungle dwellers, maniacle gnomes, drunk barbarians, lost kittens, government conspiracies...

All that and more awaits in one of the biggest, most indepth mmo's you can find. With 119 quests (and counting!) in an ever expanding, mysterious world, you'll never run out of stories, or places to explore.

Sandbox!

23 skills, 119 quests, 38 minigames, 4 official game-modes, hundreds of unofficial game-modes, and an unlimited number of account builds...

Oldschool Runescape is a completely open sandbox. Do whatever you want, whenever you want, however you want to! Want to kill a ridiculous boss for the chance at striking it rich? Go for it! Want to make clothes? Sure! How about dance with your friends by a river? Yeah sure, whatever you want!

Community!

Content polls, weekly livestream Q&A's with the devs, and more...

Oldschool Runescape only exists because the players voted for it. Major content is polled in advance in ingame pollbooths, and ideas are hashed out by devs live on twitch, or given by players on social media and taken on by developers. We run the game, but you tell us how to.
